Telehealth Consultations

Care from the
comfort of home.

We want to meet you where you are, whether that means face-to-face or in the comfort of your home, office, or wherever you may need to connect. Let us welcome you into our virtual waiting room, with no travel, at a time that works with your schedule.

Simple from Start to Finish

How it works

No downloads, no accounts, no hassle. Your appointment runs in your browser, just like a regular visit — only from wherever you are.

1

Book

Contact us by phone, email connection below, or through our online link to arrange a telehealth consultation. We'll confirm a time that works for everyone.

2

Receive your link

Shortly before your appointment, you'll receive a secure, unique link via SMS or email. Or just come here and click the Join your appointment button above. No account or download is required.

3

Join the call

We recommend arriving 5-10min before your appointment time, click your link, enter your name, view our consents, allow camera and microphone access... then relax and enjoy the waiting room. Dr Anna will welcome you in.

4

Your consultation

Speak with Dr Anna face to face. She'll review your concerns, discuss your imaging with easy to see Coviu sharing, and walk you through your options — just as she would in person.

Before You Connect

What you'll need

Our tips to help you prepare for your appointment.

A device

Computer, tablet, or smartphone — with a camera and microphone enabled.

Internet connection

A stable Wi-Fi or mobile data connection. A quiet spot at home works well.

Your Choice of Browser

Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari. No downloads or plugins required — it runs in the browser.

A private space

Find a quiet, well-lit room where you can speak freely and feel comfortable during your consultation.

Ready to Join?

You'll get a full screen view with your live link, but visit our Welcome Room here if you want to warm up to the space before hand. We hope every visit is a beautiful one.
