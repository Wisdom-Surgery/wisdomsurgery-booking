Telehealth Consultations

Care from the
comfort of home.

We want to meet you where you are, whether that means face-to-face or in the comfort of your home, office, or wherever you may need to connect. Let us welcome you into our virtual waiting room, with no travel, at a time that works with your schedule.

Book a Telehealth Appointment Join your appointment →

Simple from Start to Finish

How it works

No downloads, no accounts, no hassle. Your appointment runs in your browser, just like a regular visit — only from wherever you are.

1

Book

Contact us by phone or through our online link to arrange a telehealth consultation. We'll confirm a time that works for everyone.

2

Receive your link

Shortly before your appointment, you'll receive a secure, unique link via SMS or email. No account or download is required.

3

Join the call

At your appointment time, click your link, enter your name, and allow camera and microphone access. Our clinic will welcome you from the waiting room.

4

Your consultation

Speak with Dr Anna face to face. She'll review your concerns, discuss your imaging, and walk you through your options — just as she would in person.

Ready to join?

Your secure appointment link will have been sent to you by SMS or email. Click it to open your session — or use the button below if you received a direct link.

If you haven't received your link, please call us on 1300 111 324 and we'll resend it.

Before You Connect

What you'll need

Our tips to help you prepare for your appointment.

A device

Computer, tablet, or smartphone — with a camera and microphone enabled.

Internet connection

A stable Wi-Fi or mobile data connection. A quiet spot at home works well.

Your Choice of Browser

Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari. No downloads or plugins required — it runs in the browser.

A private space

Find a quiet, well-lit room where you can speak freely and feel comfortable during your consultation.
