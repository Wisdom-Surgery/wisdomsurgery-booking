Bulk Billing Agreement — effective 1 July 2026

About Bulk Billing

Bulk billing means your practice directs your Medicare benefit directly to Wisdom Surgery Clinic as full payment for the service provided. You have no out-of-pocket cost for the Medicare-covered component of your care.

By completing this form, you are assigning your Medicare benefit to Wisdom Surgery Clinic. This is a legal requirement under the Health Insurance Act 1973 for bulk billed services. This agreement meets the requirements of the Health Insurance Amendment (Assignment of Medicare Benefits and Other Measures) Regulations 2025, effective 1 July 2026.
