About Bulk Billing

Bulk billing means your Medicare benefit is directed directly to the treating practitioner as full payment for the service provided. You have no out-of-pocket cost for the Medicare-covered component of your appointment.

By completing this form, you are assigning your Medicare benefit to the treating practitioner. This is a legal requirement under the Health Insurance Act 1973. This agreement meets the requirements of the Health Insurance Amendment (Assignment of Medicare Benefits and Other Measures) Regulations 2025, effective 1 July 2026.

This agreement is retained by the treating practitioner for a minimum of two years from the date the Medicare claim is lodged. A copy is available on request.
