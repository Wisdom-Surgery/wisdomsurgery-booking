Medicare & Health Fund Rebates

Estimates of rebates from Medicare or Private Health Funds are provided as a guide only. Exact rebates are determined by the relevant organisation and may vary.

Patients remain responsible for payment of the full surgical fee for services provided. Once surgery is complete, a receipt will be issued for payments — typically within one week — which can then be submitted to your health fund or Medicare to receive any applicable rebates.

Validity of Quotation

Fees may increase during new calendar and financial years, indexed with the CPI, and to reflect the real costs of delivering your care.

By signing below, you lock in your fee estimate for 6 months from the date of this quotation.
